is an online blog sharing my reviews on a broad range of digital games that interest me and also my reflections of how video games could relate to the concept of ‘new media art’, and more importantly to the concept of ‘digital craftmanship’. The aim of this platform is to communicate my passion and my thinking of digital games to people with shared enthusiasms towards games, art and how they could relate to each other. It will also be a great pleasure if someone who is not a game person but also find the website interesting.

About me and my passion  >>>

Hi, my name is Zina, a current first-year computer science student at ANU. I am enthusiastic with digital game works and always feel thrilled of the immersive communication experience that this new genre of interaction has brought me into.
